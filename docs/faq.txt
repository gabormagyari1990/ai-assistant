Frequently Asked Questions

Product Returns
Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging. Returns must be initiated within 30 days of purchase. Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was received damaged or incorrect.

Shipping Information
Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping options are available for 1-2 business day delivery. International shipping times vary by destination but usually take 7-14 business days.

Payment Methods
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For security reasons, we do not accept cash or checks.

Account Management
Q: How do I reset my password?
A: To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to create a new password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please check your spam folder.

Product Information
Q: Are your products covered by warranty?
A: Yes, all our products come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer's warranty against defects. Some products may have extended warranty options available. Warranty details are included with each product and can be found on the product page.
